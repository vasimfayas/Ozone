Ozone

An NRI originally from Nandi, a small town in Koyilandy, embarked on an entrepreneurial journey by launching his own venture centered around the traditional and culturally rich product known as Arabic Coffee.

His business aims to introduce the authentic taste and heritage of Arabic coffee to a global audience, combining his roots and passion for this cherished beverage with his international experience

The objectives are to provide employment to low income groups especially women, produce food products in traditional methods, not add preservatives and to grow the business gradually assuring profit to investors


As of 2024, team Ozone and Podikkada are progressing steadily toward their goals, driven by a shared vision of success. With two fully operational manufacturing units, they have expanded their product range to over 30 high-quality offerings. This growth is supported by a vibrant team, whose dedication has led to high levels of customer satisfaction.

In an effort to bring quality products closer to customers, Podikkadas have been opened in every town, offering a wide range of choices that cater to local preferences. This strategic expansion strengthens the brand's local presence and builds trust within the communities they serve.

Looking ahead, Ozone and Podikkada have developed innovative strategies and action plans aimed at capturing a significant market share within the next five years. By focusing on understanding customer needs, leveraging emerging trends, and embracing technology, they are well-positioned for growth.

At the same time, major infrastructure development projects are in progress to create state-of-the-art facilities. These projects are designed to enhance operational efficiency and bolster the confidence of capital investors, ensuring a solid foundation for future success and long-term sustainability in the competitive market.

Our products consist of a wide range of daily-use food powders, such as rice powder, chicken masala, mandi masala, putt powder, and more, all made with fresh ingredients. We take pride in ensuring that our products are crafted without the use of any preservatives, preserving the natural flavors and nutritional value. Whether you're preparing traditional dishes or trying something new, our food powders are designed to enhance the taste and quality of your meals while offering a healthier, more wholesome option for your kitchen.
