Ozone

An NRI originally from Nandi, a small town in Koyilandy, embarked on an entrepreneurial journey by launching his own venture centered around the traditional and culturally rich product known as Arabic Coffee.

His business aims to introduce the authentic taste and heritage of Arabic coffee to a global audience, combining his roots and passion for this cherished beverage with his international experience
